WE ARE EDUNEXT

What is Edunext ?

Edunext is a student community in kerala aimed to develop interpersonal skills in school students through workshops and bootcamps.We make peer to peer learning clusters among school students.

Why Edunext ?

Moreover we are a team of passionate IOT aspirants, later it has been turned into a teaching community of young IOT develpors and today evolved as Edunext community.

How we Operate ?

We conduct workshops in schools and colleges on Internet of Things(IOT), through we'll make them able to comit projects and give access to become the shareholders of our projects.

OUR ACTIVITIES

IOT Workshop

Internet of Things is something an intresting universe of sensors and microcontrollers.It's intresting and creative to deal with IOT projects.We provide one day workshop onbasic working of arduino and raspberry boards for school students.

Web Design Botcamp

Building website are often considerd are sometimes ridiculous,but it's actually cool to create stuffs on web. We provide a series of web design sessions for the students and make them able to build and host a simple portfolio website using HTML and CSS

Debate Competitions

Debate is something that makes our surroundings a war zone for a small period of time. It's quite interesting to debate with someone with weird thoughts. We conduct debate sessions amoung students which uplift their skills in public speaking and debating.
